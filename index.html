    About EC Rater

    Built by a student,
    for every student

    I built EC Rater because I was frustrated. Every college prep resource either gave empty encouragement or vague advice. Nobody would just tell me the truth about whether my extracurriculars were actually good enough.

    So I built the tool I wished I had. EC Rater uses real AI to compare your EC to real students who got in. It respects you enough to be honest, and cares enough to show you how to improve.

    100% free, built with passion, made for every student who just wants to know where they actually stand.
